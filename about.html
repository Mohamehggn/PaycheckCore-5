Our Story

Founded in 2026 with a Purpose

PaycheckCore was founded in early 2026 by a group of payroll specialists, software engineers, and personal finance educators who were tired of seeing employees confused by their pay stubs. After years of answering the same question from friends and family — "Why is my take-home pay so much less than my salary?" — we decided to build the tool we wished existed.

The founding team met while working on enterprise payroll software and noticed a gap: while large companies had sophisticated systems to calculate withholdings, individual employees had no reliable way to estimate their net pay or understand deductions before accepting a job offer or requesting changes to their W-4 or TD1 forms.

We launched PaycheckCore from our headquarters in Oil City, Pennsylvania with a commitment to accessibility, accuracy, and education. Today, we serve hundreds of thousands of users each month across the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada, helping them plan budgets, negotiate salaries, and understand their compensation with confidence.

Comprehensive Paycheck Calculators and Tax Tools

Free Paycheck Calculator for USA, UK, and Canada

Understanding your take-home pay is the foundation of personal financial planning. PaycheckCore offers state-of-the-art paycheck calculators designed for employees, contractors, and employers in the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada. Our tools account for the latest federal and state tax brackets, National Insurance contributions, CPP and EI deductions, provincial taxes, and voluntary deductions like 401(k), pension plans, and health insurance premiums. Each calculator is updated for the 2026 tax year to ensure you receive accurate estimates that reflect current legislation.

For US taxpayers, our salary calculator handles federal income tax withholding based on IRS Publication 15-T, FICA taxes including Social Security at 6.2% and Medicare at 1.45%, state income tax for all 50 states, and local taxes where applicable. You can model different pay frequencies including weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, monthly, and annual. The calculator also supports pre-tax deductions such as traditional 401(k) contributions, HSA contributions, and Section 125 cafeteria plans, showing you exactly how these benefits reduce your taxable income and increase your net pay.

UK Salary Calculator and Income Tax Tools

Our UK paycheck calculator provides detailed breakdowns for employees paid under PAYE. We calculate Income Tax based on the 2026/27 UK tax bands for England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland, including the personal allowance, basic rate, higher rate, and additional rate thresholds. National Insurance contributions are computed using the correct Class 1 rates for employees, and we include options for both standard and director NI calculations. Users can also factor in student loan repayments for Plan 1, Plan 2, Plan 4, and Postgraduate loans, as well as pension contributions under auto-enrolment rules with tax relief applied.

The UK tool helps contractors working through umbrella companies and limited companies estimate take-home pay after corporation tax, dividends, and IR35 considerations. By entering your gross salary, tax code, and other deductions, you receive an instant estimate of your monthly and annual net pay, making it easier to budget and compare job offers across different regions of the UK.

Canada Paycheck Calculator with Provincial Tax Rates

Canadian users benefit from our comprehensive Canada salary calculator that incorporates both federal and provincial tax rates for 2026. We support all provinces and territories, including Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta, Quebec, and others, with accurate calculations for basic personal amounts and graduated tax brackets. The calculator automatically deducts Canada Pension Plan contributions and Employment Insurance premiums up to the annual maximums, while also handling Quebec Pension Plan and QPIP for Quebec residents.

Whether you're completing a TD1 form, evaluating a new employment offer, or planning RRSP contributions, our Canadian tax calculator shows how each factor affects your net income. You can adjust for pay periods, bonus payments, taxable benefits, and union dues to get a realistic picture of your earnings. The tool is especially useful for newcomers to Canada who need to understand the difference between gross and net pay in their first jobs.

Why Use PaycheckCore Instead of Manual Calculations?

Tax laws are complex and change annually. Manually calculating withholdings using tax tables and formulas is time-consuming and prone to error. PaycheckCore automates this process using verified algorithms and official government sources. Our engineers monitor updates from the IRS, HMRC, and CRA to ensure every tax rate, allowance, and deduction limit is current. This commitment to accuracy means you can trust our results when making important decisions like buying a house, adjusting your W-4 allowances, or setting up salary sacrifice arrangements.

Beyond basic paycheck calculations, PaycheckCore offers educational resources explaining how taxes work. Our guides cover topics such as marginal vs effective tax rates, the difference between pre-tax and post-tax deductions, how bonuses are taxed, and strategies to optimize your take-home pay legally. We believe financial literacy is a right, not a privilege, and our content is written in plain language without jargon.

Privacy and Security You Can Trust

We understand that salary information is sensitive. That's why PaycheckCore operates with a privacy-first approach. All calculations are performed locally in your web browser using JavaScript. Your salary, deductions, and personal details are never transmitted to our servers or stored in any database. We do not require account creation, email sign-ups, or personal identification to use our calculators. This architecture ensures your data remains completely private while still delivering instant, accurate results. Our website is served over HTTPS and complies with Google AdSense policies for user safety and content quality.

Our goal is to become the most trusted resource for paycheck and tax calculations worldwide. We continuously expand our tools based on user feedback, adding new features like overtime calculators, commission estimators, and self-employment tax tools. If you have suggestions for improvements or find a discrepancy, our support team is ready to help.
