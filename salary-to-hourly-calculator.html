How to Use the Salary to Hourly Calculator

Convert your annual salary to an hourly wage. This calculator accounts for PTO, holidays, and actual hours worked to give you the most accurate hourly equivalent of your salary.

Step 1: Enter Your Annual Salary

Input your gross yearly salary before taxes. Example: Enter 75000 for a $75,000 salary.

Step 2: Set Your Work Schedule

Enter hours per week (typically 40) and paid weeks per year. Standard is 52 weeks, but use 50 if you take 2 weeks unpaid, 48 for 4 weeks unpaid.

Step 3: Add Paid Time Off

Include PTO days and paid holidays. Standard US is 10 PTO + 10 holidays = 20 days. This calculates your TRUE hourly rate based on days you actually work.

Why Convert Salary to Hourly?

  • Compare Job Offers: See if a $70k salary beats a $35/hour contract role
  • Freelance Pricing: Know your hourly value to set consulting or freelance rates
  • Overtime Value: Understand what your time is worth for side projects
  • Salary Negotiation: Break down annual salary into hourly to negotiate better
  • Time Management: Know if that 2-hour meeting is worth $100 of your time

Pro Tip: True Hourly Rate

Your "true" hourly rate is higher when you factor in PTO and holidays. If you make $80,000 but get 4 weeks PTO + 2 weeks holidays, you're actually making $41.67/hour, not $38.46/hour. This calculator shows both.
