How to Use the Hourly to Salary Calculator

This calculator converts your hourly wage into an equivalent annual salary. Perfect for comparing job offers, negotiating raises, or budgeting your yearly income.

Step 1: Enter Your Hourly Wage

Input your current hourly rate. For example, if you make $25/hour, enter 25.00.

Step 2: Set Your Work Schedule

Enter hours worked per week (standard is 40) and weeks per year. Use 52 for full year, or 50 if you take 2 weeks unpaid vacation, 48 for 4 weeks off.

Step 3: Add Overtime (Optional)

If you regularly work overtime at 1.5x pay, enter average overtime hours per week. The calculator applies time-and-a-half rates automatically.

Why This Matters

  • Job Comparison: Compare hourly vs salary job offers accurately
  • Salary Negotiation: Know your true annual value when asking for raises
  • Budget Planning: Understand your yearly gross income for loans, mortgages, and budgets
  • Tax Planning: Estimate your tax bracket based on annual income

Common Conversions

  • $15/hour = $31,200/year (40 hrs, 52 weeks)
  • $20/hour = $41,600/year (40 hrs, 52 weeks)
  • $25/hour = $52,000/year (40 hrs, 52 weeks)
  • $30/hour = $62,400/year (40 hrs, 52 weeks)
  • $50/hour = $104,000/year (40 hrs, 52 weeks)

Note: This calculates gross annual income before taxes, 401k, health insurance, and other deductions. Use our Paycheck Calculator to find your actual take-home pay.
